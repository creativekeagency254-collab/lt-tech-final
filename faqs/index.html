Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for checkout, delivery and order support.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery depends on your location. Nairobi orders can be faster, while upcountry deliveries usually take longer.

Can I pay securely online?

Yes. Checkout is supported by Paystack. You can also request assisted checkout through WhatsApp.

How do I track my order?

Use the Track Order page and share your order number with support for status updates.
